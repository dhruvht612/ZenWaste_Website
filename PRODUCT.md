# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Operations directors and GMs at waste haulers (CR&R is the pilot). Practical, skeptical of vendor hype, evaluating on evidence — uptime, proof of service, cost. They view this page on a laptop, in an office, mid-workday, usually alongside a live product demo run by the GoFleet sales team. It is a sales/demo surface, not an inbound-marketing funnel.

## Product Purpose

ZenduWaste is GoFleet's waste-fleet intelligence platform: AI pickup confirmation, contamination detection, and missed-stop exception dispatch — running on the ZenCam cameras already mounted on the customer's trucks (zero new hardware). The page exists to prove that claim to a skeptical operator in one scroll. Success = a booked walkthrough on the prospect's own routes.

## Brand Personality

Credible, industrial, in-control. The voice of a dispatch room, not a startup. Evidence over adjectives: every claim is shown as a working product surface (live feed, route map, camera frame), never as decoration. Three words: operational, verifiable, calm.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages: gradient text, aurora blobs, tilt cards, count-up stat grids, uppercase tracked eyebrows over every section.
- "Eco-startup" green pastel waste-industry clichés.
- Telematics competitor sites (Samsara/Geotab) — we are the intelligence layer, not another GPS dashboard.

## Design Principles

1. **The product is the imagery.** Every visual is a rendering of the real Command Center, route map, or ZenCam evidence frame — no stock art, no abstract illustration.
2. **The canvas is the brand.** ZenduONE's deep-navy canvas gradient (#010658 → #050712) with a floating white inner container is the app's spatial signature; the site opens and closes inside it. Dark = the 4 a.m. shift, light = the routes running in daylight.
3. **Machine voice for machine facts.** Timestamps, confidence scores, route IDs are set in mono; human copy in Inter. The contrast is the type system.
4. **One accent, blue only.** Primary #136AB6 carries the brand; semantic green/amber/red carry meaning; nothing else.
5. **Motion means something is running.** Animation is reserved for things that are genuinely live (the feed ticking, the truck moving along the route) — never applied as entrance garnish to static content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1 on both the navy canvas and white surfaces. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternative (live feed rests, truck parks, reveals become instant). Semantic landmarks, focus rings on all interactive elements, comparison table remains a real `<table>`.
